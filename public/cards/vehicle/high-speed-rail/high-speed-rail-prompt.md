# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: undefined
Category: 交通工具

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Vehicle Category Template

## Accent Color

* Industrial Red #B45A4A

## Identity

* Transportation and engineering encyclopedia profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Accurate side view, three-quarter view, cutaway, or structural diagram
* 3–5 detached structural or performance detail panels

## Information Modules

* 载具档案
* 结构组成
* 动力系统
* 性能参数
* 设计特点
* 使用场景
* 发展沿革
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 速度性能
* 工程复杂度
* 实用程度
* 历史影响
* 识别度

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive structural components appropriate to the vehicle
* Examples: body, cockpit, cabin, propulsion, chassis, wings, hull, wheels, tracks
* Do not show components absent from the subject

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Size comparison
* Powertrain diagram
* Evolution timeline

## Failure Prevention

* No commercial vehicle advertising
* No showroom photography
* No exaggerated speed effects
* No invented specifications
* Engineering structure must remain plausible
