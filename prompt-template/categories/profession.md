# Profession Category Template

## Accent Color

* Professional Blue #4D7393

## Identity

* Career and social-role encyclopedia profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Representative work scene, tools, environment, and workflow
* 3–5 small task, tool, skill, or workplace callouts around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 职业档案
* 核心职责
* 工作环境
* 专业技能
* 培养路径
* 常用工具
* 行业发展
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 专业门槛
* 体力要求
* 创造程度
* 社会需求
* 工作压力

## Observation Insets

* 工具
* 场景
* 技能
* 流程
* 装备

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Career pathway
* Workflow diagram
* Skill composition

## Failure Prevention

* No recruitment advertising
* No salary-promise language
* No heroic or stereotyped portrayal
* Avoid gender or cultural stereotypes
* Present typical patterns, not universal claims
