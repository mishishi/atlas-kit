# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: undefined
Category: 天体

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Space Object Category Template

## Accent Color

* Cosmic Blue #486A9A

## Identity

* Astronomy encyclopedia celestial-object profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Scientific celestial rendering with scale, structure, or orbital context
* 3–5 detached observable or inferred feature panels

## Information Modules

* 天体档案
* 结构组成
* 轨道参数
* 物理特征
* 形成演化
* 观测历史
* 探测研究
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 体量尺度
* 观测难度
* 科研价值
* 极端程度
* 公众知名度

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 scientifically appropriate observable or inferred features
* Examples: surface, atmosphere, rings, jets, magnetic field, internal structure, orbit, spectrum
* Do not invent a solid surface or atmosphere

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Scale comparison
* Orbit diagram
* Structure cross-section

## Failure Prevention

* No cinematic space poster
* No fantasy nebula effects
* No invented missions or measurements
* Distinguish observation from scientific inference
* Preserve scale and orbital plausibility
