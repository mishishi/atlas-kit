# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: Overlord
Category: 动漫

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Anime Category Template

## Accent Color

* Deep Purple — #6E628C

## Identity

* Animation and anime encyclopedia profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Representative animation-style composition showing main characters, world setting, and key visual motifs
* Present the subject as an encyclopedia entry, not a promotional poster
* Preserve the recognizable visual DNA of the work without copying a specific official poster
* 3–5 detached character, setting, or motif detail panels around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 作品档案
* 世界观设定
* 主要角色
* 角色关系
* 美术风格
* 制作演出
* 主题表达
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 世界观完整度
* 角色辨识度
* 美术表现
* 叙事影响
* 传播广度

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive anime-specific details appropriate to the subject
* Examples: main character, costume detail, symbolic object, setting element, creature, faction emblem, visual motif, animation style cue
* Prefer visually different details
* Avoid duplicate character portraits
* Do not mix anime, manga, game, or live-action versions unless relevant
* Short labels only

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact analytical visualizations
* Character relationship map
* Faction or world-setting diagram
* Story arc timeline
* Visual motif strip
* Minimal labels only

## Failure Prevention

* No promotional anime poster
* No streaming platform interface
* No fake official logo, studio logo, watermark, or QR code
* Do not copy a specific official poster composition
* Do not mix unrelated characters or franchises
* Do not merge anime, manga, game, or live-action continuities unless relevant
* Avoid generic anime faces when the subject has recognizable character designs
