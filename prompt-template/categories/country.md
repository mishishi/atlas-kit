# Country Category Template

## Accent Color

* Nationally representative color, used with restraint

## Identity

* Geographic and cultural encyclopedia country profile page

## Hero Illustration

* National overview combining terrain, map silhouette, representative landscape, and cultural symbols
* 3–5 detached geographic or cultural detail panels

## Information Modules

* 国家档案
* 地理环境
* 历史沿革
* 政治体制
* 人口社会
* 经济产业
* 文化特色
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 国土规模
* 文化影响
* 经济体量
* 地理多样性
* 全球影响

## Observation Insets

* 地形
* 城市
* 建筑
* 服饰
* 象征

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Location map
* Terrain profile
* Population or industry composition

## Failure Prevention

* No tourism advertising
* No nationalist propaganda
* No postcard collage
* No political persuasion
* Balance physical geography, population, economy, history, and culture
* Do not let flags or national symbols dominate the page
* Avoid presenting disputed political claims as uncontested facts
