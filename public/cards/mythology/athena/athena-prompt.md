# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 雅典娜
Category: 神话

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Mythology Category Template

## Accent Color

* Mythic Indigo #5D5A8A

## Identity

* Mythology and folklore encyclopedia profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Symbolic mythological figure, creature, scene, or cosmological diagram
* 3–5 detached attribute or narrative detached detail panels around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 神话档案
* 文化来源
* 核心形象
* 主要故事
* 象征意义
* 相关神祇
* 后世影响
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 文化影响
* 传播广度
* 象征复杂度
* 叙事知名度
* 后世改编度

## Observation Insets

* 形象
* 器物
* 标志
* 场景
* 关系

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Genealogy map
* Myth cycle
* Cultural distribution map

## Failure Prevention

* No fantasy game poster
* No heroic propaganda
* Do not merge unrelated myth systems
* Prioritize the earliest or culturally established tradition
* Clearly separate original mythology from later adaptations
* Do not use modern game or film costume designs as historical canon
* Maintain cultural neutrality and respect
