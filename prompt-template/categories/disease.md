# Disease Category Template

## Accent Color

* Clinical Teal #3F7F7A

## Identity

* Medical encyclopedia disease profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Clinical anatomy, pathogen, mechanism, or symptom-distribution diagram
* 3–5 small mechanism, organ, symptom, or diagnostic callouts around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 疾病档案
* 病因机制
* 主要症状
* 传播途径
* 风险因素
* 诊断方式
* 预防治疗
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 严重程度
* 传播风险
* 诊断难度
* 治疗成熟度
* 预防可行性

## Observation Insets

* 病原
* 器官
* 症状
* 机制
* 检测

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Mechanism flow
* Body-system map
* Prevention or treatment pathway

## Failure Prevention

* No fear-based medical imagery
* No graphic suffering
* No diagnosis of individuals
* No exaggerated cure claims
* Present general educational information only
