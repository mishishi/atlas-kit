# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 菲律宾
Category: 国家

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Country Category Template

## Accent Color

* Nationally representative color, used with restraint

## Identity

* Geographic and cultural encyclopedia country profile page

## Hero Illustration

* National overview combining terrain, map silhouette, representative landscape, and cultural symbols
* 3–5 detached geographic or cultural detail panels

## Information Modules

* 国家档案
* 地理环境
* 历史沿革
* 政治体制
* 人口社会
* 经济产业
* 文化特色
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 国土规模
* 文化影响
* 经济体量
* 地理多样性
* 全球影响

## Observation Insets

* 地形
* 城市
* 建筑
* 服饰
* 象征

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Location map
* Terrain profile
* Population or industry composition

## Failure Prevention

* No tourism advertising
* No nationalist propaganda
* No postcard collage
* No political persuasion
* Balance physical geography, population, economy, history, and culture
* Do not let flags or national symbols dominate the page
* Avoid presenting disputed political claims as uncontested facts
