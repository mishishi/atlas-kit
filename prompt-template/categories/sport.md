# Sport Category Template

## Accent Color

* Athletic Green #4F8A62

## Identity

* Sports encyclopedia discipline profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Clear athletic action, field layout, equipment, or technical posture
* 3–5 small movement, equipment, or rule callouts around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 项目档案
* 起源沿革
* 场地器材
* 核心规则
* 技术动作
* 赛事体系
* 体能要求
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 体能强度
* 技术难度
* 对抗程度
* 全球普及度
* 观赏性

## Observation Insets

* 动作
* 器材
* 场地
* 姿势
* 战术

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Field diagram
* Movement sequence
* Competition structure

## Failure Prevention

* No event-promotion poster
* No sports-brand advertising
* No exaggerated action effects
* Keep rules and equipment accurate
* Avoid depicting one athlete as the whole sport
