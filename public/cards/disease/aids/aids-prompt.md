# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 艾滋病
Category: 疾病

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Disease Category Template

## Accent Color

* Clinical Teal #3F7F7A

## Identity

* Medical encyclopedia disease profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Clinical anatomy, mechanism, affected-system, or symptom-distribution diagram
* 3–5 detached medically relevant detail panels

## Information Modules

* 疾病档案
* 病因机制
* 主要表现
* 风险因素
* 影响系统
* 诊断方式
* 预防治疗
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 严重程度
* 传播风险
* 诊断难度
* 治疗成熟度
* 预防可行性

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 medically relevant features
* Examples: affected organ, cellular mechanism, symptom pattern, diagnostic sign, pathogen when applicable
* Do not imply an infectious cause unless applicable

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Mechanism flow
* Body-system map
* Prevention or treatment pathway

## Failure Prevention

* No fear-based medical imagery
* No graphic suffering
* No diagnosis of individuals
* No exaggerated cure claims
* Present general educational information only
