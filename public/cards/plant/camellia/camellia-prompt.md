# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 山茶花
Category: 植物

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Plant Category Template

## Accent Color
* Forest Green #4A8C5C

## Identity
* Botanical field-guide specimen page

## Hero Illustration

* Complete botanical specimen with visible structure
* 3–5 detached botanical structure or cross-section panels

## Information Modules
* 分类阶元
* 形态描述
* 生长周期
* 分布产地
* 生态需求
* 用途价值
* 毒性提示
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions
* 观赏价值
* 药用价值
* 食用价值
* 种植难度
* 生态重要性

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive botanical structures appropriate to the plant
* Examples: leaf, flower, fruit, seed, cone, root, stem, bark, spore
* Prefer visually different structures
* Avoid showing organs absent from the subject

## Visualization Strip
* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Growth cycle chart
* Structure diagram
* Distribution map

## Failure Prevention
* No gardening promotion
* No decorative flower poster
* No exaggerated medicinal claims
* Toxicity must be clearly indicated
