# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 大象 (动物)
Category: 动物

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Animal Category Template

## Accent Color
* Warm Amber #E07840

## Identity
* Natural-history species profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Scientific full-body species illustration
* 3–5 detached anatomical close-up panels around the hero illustration

## Information Modules
* 分类阶元
* 形态特征
* 栖息环境
* 食性行为
* 繁殖习性
* 保护现状
* 分布地图
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions
* 体型
* 攻击性
* 稀有度
* 适应力
* 人类熟悉度

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive anatomical features appropriate to the species
* Prefer visually different features
* Avoid duplicate or near-duplicate close-ups
* Examples: head, eyes, beak, teeth, limbs, wings, fur, feathers, scales, tail
* Do not show features absent from the species

## Visualization Strip
* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Range map
* Human size comparison
* Ecological niche

## Failure Prevention
* Scientific illustration style
* No wildlife photography poster
* No cartoon or mascot
* No anthropomorphic styling
* No emotional conservation messaging
