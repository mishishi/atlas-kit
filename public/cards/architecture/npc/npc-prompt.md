# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 国家大剧院
Category: 建筑

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Architecture Category Template

## Accent Color

* Sandstone Gold #B88952

## Identity

* Architectural history and design encyclopedia page

## Hero Illustration

* Elevation, axonometric view, cutaway, or site-context illustration
* 3–5 detached structural, material, or decorative detail panels

## Information Modules

* 建筑档案
* 地理位置
* 历史背景
* 空间结构
* 建筑风格
* 材料工艺
* 功能用途
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 历史价值
* 结构复杂度
* 艺术表现
* 文化象征
* 保存程度

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive architectural details
* Examples: facade, roof, entrance, structure, window, column, dome, ornament, material
* Do not impose classical elements on unrelated architecture

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Floor plan
* Structural section
* Construction timeline

## Failure Prevention

* No real-estate advertising
* No luxury-property brochure style
* No cinematic architectural rendering
* Do not mix unrelated architectural elements
* Preserve structural and historical plausibility
