# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 千与千寻
Category: 电影

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Movie Category Template

## Accent Color

* Cinema Red #A34848

## Identity

* Film history and cinema encyclopedia profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Representative cinematic composition, storyboard analysis, or visual-language study
* Present the film as an encyclopedia subject, not a promotional poster
* 3–5 detached cinematic analysis panels

## Information Modules

* 影片档案
* 创作背景
* 剧情概要
* 主要人物
* 视听风格
* 主题表达
* 影响评价
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 艺术影响
* 叙事完成度
* 视听创新
* 传播广度
* 时代代表性

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinct visual elements
* Examples: composition, lighting, costume, prop, setting, recurring motif
* Avoid repeating similar character portraits

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Story structure
* Character relationship map
* Production or release timeline

## Failure Prevention

* No promotional movie poster
* No box-office marketing
* No fabricated awards or quotations
* Avoid excessive spoilers
* Maintain neutral film analysis
