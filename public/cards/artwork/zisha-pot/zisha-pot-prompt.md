# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 紫砂壶
Category: 艺术品

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Artwork Category Template

## Accent Color

* Gallery Ochre #B47A3C

## Identity

* Art-history encyclopedia artwork profile page

## Hero Illustration

* One faithful full reproduction of the artwork
* Preserve the original composition, proportions, orientation, and dominant palette
* Do not reinterpret, rearrange, simplify, or add elements
* Compact museum-catalogue presentation

## Information Modules

* 作品档案
* 作者背景
* 创作背景
* 构图分析
* 色彩语言
* 技法材料
* 历史影响
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 艺术影响
* 技法复杂度
* 视觉识别度
* 文化价值
* 传播广度

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up crops taken directly from the artwork
* Show genuine details from different regions of the original composition
* Prefer visually distinct details
* Do not invent alternative versions or unrelated symbolic illustrations
* Short labels only

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact analytical visualizations
* Prefer composition diagram, restrained color palette, or provenance timeline
* No more than 4–5 timeline points
* Minimal labels only

## Failure Prevention

* No reinterpretation of the original artwork
* No invented visual elements
* No decorative poster treatment
* No auction or exhibition advertising
* No oversized infographic icons
* Do not invent provenance, dimensions, or quotations
