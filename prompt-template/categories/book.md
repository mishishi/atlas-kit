# Book Category Template

## Accent Color

* Library Burgundy #8A4B55

## Identity

* Literary encyclopedia and publication profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Book cover, manuscript, symbolic scene, or archival publication composition
* 3–5 small work-related callouts around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 作品档案
* 作者背景
* 创作背景
* 内容概要
* 主题思想
* 叙事结构
* 影响评价
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 文学影响
* 思想深度
* 传播广度
* 阅读难度
* 时代代表性

## Observation Insets

* 封面
* 手稿
* 人物
* 场景
* 象征

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Narrative structure
* Character relationship map
* Publication timeline

## Failure Prevention

* No commercial book advertising
* No bestseller promotion
* No fabricated quotations
* Avoid excessive plot spoilers
* Maintain neutral literary analysis
