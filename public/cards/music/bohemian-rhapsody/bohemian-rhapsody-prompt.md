# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: Bohemian Rhapsody
Category: 音乐

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Music Category Template

## Accent Color

* Deep Purple — #6E628C

## Identity

* Music encyclopedia and sound-culture profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Representative music composition showing performer, instrument, score, album, stage, or sonic structure
* Present the subject as an encyclopedia entry, not a concert poster or album advertisement
* 3–5 detached music-detail panels around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 音乐档案
* 创作背景
* 曲式结构
* 声音语言
* 编曲结构
* 主题表达
* 传播影响
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 艺术影响
* 旋律识别度
* 技术复杂度
* 传播广度
* 时代代表性

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive musical or visual details appropriate to the subject
* Examples: instrument, score, rhythm pattern, motif, performer, album cover detail, stage element, cultural symbol
* Prefer visually different details
* Avoid showing unrelated instruments, performers, or modern adaptations unless relevant
* Short labels only

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact analytical visualizations
* Song structure
* Timeline
* Arrangement map
* Rhythm or motif diagram
* Minimal labels only

## Failure Prevention

* No concert poster
* No album marketing
* No streaming platform interface
* No fabricated lyrics, awards, chart rankings, or quotations
* Do not confuse the musical work with a single performer unless the subject requires it
* Distinguish original work, performance version, and later adaptation
