# Encyclopedia Infographic

Theme: 兔子
Category: 宠物

STRICT 9:16 vertical page.

## Style

* Natural History Museum
* DK Encyclopedia
* National Geographic
* Warm ivory paper background
* Subtle paper texture
* Thin dividers
* Rounded information panels
* Single accent color

## Layout

* Header
* Hero Illustration with 3–5 observation insets
* Information Grid
* Visualization Strip
* Summary Bar

## Header

* Large serif Chinese title (name only)
* Italic subtitle (scientific name, foreign name, or alias)
* 3–5 pill tags
* Thin divider line
* Faint specimen-style decoration in the upper-right corner
* No taxonomy, distribution, conservation, or other data text in the header

## Observation Insets

* 3–5 detached close-up panels
* Short labels only
* Uniform panel size
* Balanced placement around the hero illustration
* Prefer visually different features
* No arrows, leader lines, or connector lines
* Use spatial proximity to imply association

## Information Grid

* 2 equal columns × 4 rows
* 2–4 short facts per module
* Similar content density across panels
* Align titles, icons, and text consistently
* No numbered module badges

## Summary Bar

* One single-line encyclopedia-style conclusion
* Strictly 15–20 Chinese characters
* One judgment only
* Centered text with small linear icons on both sides
* Slightly darker background
* Thin dividers and subtle decorative elements
* No heading

## Text

* Simplified Chinese
* Clear readable titles and labels
* Short facts
* Approximate values
* No long paragraphs

## Output Quality

* Sharp text
* Fine details
* Clean edges

## Failure Prevention

* No poster
* No advertisement
* No marketing language
* No cinematic composition
* No large empty areas

# Pet Category Template

## Accent Color
* Soft Orange #E8845A

## Identity
* Breed-standard companion animal profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Accurate breed or species-standard pose with clear identifying features
* 3–5 detached breed or species feature panels

## Information Modules
* 品种档案
* 体型外貌
* 性格特点
* 饲养要点
* 健康数据
* 训练适性
* 适合人群
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 活跃度
* 亲人度
* 饲养难度
* 环境要求
* 互动程度

## Observation Insets

* Select 3–5 distinctive breed or species features
* Adapt to mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, or aquatic pets
* Prefer visually different features
* Avoid mammal-only anatomy when not applicable

## Visualization Strip
* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Size comparison
* Breed traits
* Care schedule

## Failure Prevention
* No pet-shop advertising
* No anthropomorphic styling
* No social-media pet aesthetics
* No internet pet slang
