# Artwork Category Template

## Accent Color

* Gallery Ochre #B47A3C

## Identity

* Art-history encyclopedia artwork profile page

## Hero Illustration

* Faithful artwork reproduction or museum-style visual study
* 3–5 small composition, technique, or detail callouts around the hero illustration

## Information Modules

* 作品档案
* 作者背景
* 创作背景
* 构图分析
* 色彩语言
* 技法材料
* 历史影响
* 快速评分卡

## Rating Dimensions

* 艺术影响
* 技法复杂度
* 视觉识别度
* 文化价值
* 传播广度

## Observation Insets

* 构图
* 色彩
* 笔触
* 材料
* 细节

## Visualization Strip

* 1–2 compact visualizations
* Composition diagram
* Color palette
* Provenance timeline

## Failure Prevention

* No auction advertising
* No gallery promotion
* No decorative poster treatment
* Do not invent provenance or dimensions
* Preserve the artwork’s visual character
